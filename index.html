Fall in Love with "Yeongdeungpo" (FLY)

Description

To revitalize the area, we designed the urban regeneration plan for the old secluded urban area 'Yeoungdeungpo, Seoul, Korea.' We conducted site and spatial analysis and analyzed the existing government's regional plan. In addition, through SWOT analysis, we examined what is needed in the region as a whole. The area was entirely old, specialized in manufacturing, and there was an abandoned overpass, so we desinged plans to renovate the facade of manufacturing buildings and put green open spaces and workshop streets which can vitalize manufacturing. Also, we planned to refurbish abandoned factories, overpass, and underneath the overpass to cafes and parks. And we made diverse goals and strategies and applied various cases to help successful urban regeneration. Finally, we embodied the buildings we planned and rendered them.
