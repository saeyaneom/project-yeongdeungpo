The New Complex City “Sindang”

Description

This project aims to design an urban redevelopment plan in "Singdang-dong, Seoul, Korea," an aged old city. Through the analysis of commercial districts in the area, connection with surrounding areas, population, transportation, spatial structure, topography, and SWOT analysis, we tried to maintain the original identity of the area's identity and tried to supplement the shortcomings. The area was known for the Korean food 'Tteokbokki,' so we designed plans to retain the concept, the commercial district of the food town and rebuild the old buildings. Instead of the dense urban landscape, we planned to add an open space that did not exist and to further revitalize the commercial district by adding residential and commercial areas. Then we designed a land use plan, a district plan, and the new buildings for the entire area based on the plan and rendered them through Twinmotion.
